The beginning of love...

In 2022, at Royal University, I was walking with my friend on the campus grounds and I noticed a girl sitting alone, enjoying an ice cream. She was incredibly beautiful, with long, flowing hair that attracted my heart. As I walked past her, I was not pay much attention at first. However, a few seconds later, have something made me to look back at her. After hesitating for a moment, I decided to take a picture of her, wanting to capture that fleeting moment of beauty. With my heart racing, I gathered my courage and walked over to her. She looked up, surprised but smiled back warmly. We struck up a conversation, and as we talked, I learned that her name was Seangna and she was studying at RUFA. Before we parted aways, I ask for her contact. She smiled and handed me her number, making my heart skip a beat. That day marked the beginning of an unexpected and beautiful connection.
